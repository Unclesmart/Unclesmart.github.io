DEPTH OF LEADERSHIP

Some company leaders have been around too long. What they do has less to do with making good decisions as it does as making habit driven decisions. When leaders are unable to listen to new ideas, ways of thinking or methods for conducting business, they are no longer leading but merely stagnant. They cease to keep the company moving forward in a dynamic way and instead stand in the way of movement.
